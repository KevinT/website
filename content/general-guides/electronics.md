### Connectors

- 3.5 mm right angle, male audio jacks with bare wire
- 3.5 mm female panel mount audio jack
- DC female panel mount barel jack
- 5 volt 2.5 amp micro USB power supply
- 12 volt, 1 amp DC barrel jack power supply


### Wiring

- Breadbord
- Perf board
- Perma proto board
